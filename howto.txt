Pokeship Instructions
1. Hide your Pokemon somewhere in the grass. Click to place them, click again to remove them.
2. Select a .battle file to load the computer's hidden Pokemon.
3. Start the game!
4. Guess where the computer's Pokemon are hidden before the computer guesses where yours are. If the computer correctly guesses where your Pokemon is, a Pokeball icon will appear. If you correctly guess where the computer's Pokemon is, the Pokemon will appear.
